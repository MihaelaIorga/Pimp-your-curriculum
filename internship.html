Internship

Knowledge

During your internship, you will acquire quick knowledge in a fast moving enviroment that you would otherwise not get with conventional studying. How to solve tasks and efficiently generate solutions in the real world is usually followed by strict deadlines, which you will learn how to overcome.

Skills

Skills that you aquire during your internship are skills that designers share amongst each other to better themselves and better and assist others. These are not always included in the conventional learning, and is a great way to get a broad spectrum of tricks and skills that can be applied to day to day work.

Competencies

As an intern, you will learn how to competently execute tasks on deadline and deliver results that are needed even under strict pressure. This is what separates good designers from the rest.

Assessment

Your exam score during your internship is assessed almost exactly the same as previous semesters given the fact that your performance in your internship company is taken into consideration, as well as feedback of your superiors and peers.

Rules of completion

You are considered to have passed all criteria of becoming a multimedia student by proving you have mastered requirements in given core areas. You are assessed internally, according to the 7-point grading scale.The final report is going to extend to 45 +15 pages per additional group members. Additionally, you need to have a “problem statement” which indicates a problem that can be solved with the skills learned, provided the “problem statement” is approved by your teachers. You may also prove competencies in this field by starting your own business.
